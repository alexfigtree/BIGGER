How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles of the same size touch, they merge into one and become BIGGER!

About: BIGGER is game is made by Alex Higuera, based on 2048 and Threes. It was made with young children in mind, to help them learn the conceptual differences between big and small. No math necessary!
